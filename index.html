Our Services

Team Building

We would help you build an amazing engineering team. To achieve your vision you need a team with you that executes like a well oiled machine. We help you vet and hire and manage the engineering team that will help you in this goal

System Architecture

We help you design and architect your system. There are so many things to consider when building a product in this brave new world. Should you go the microservices route? Should you use container orchestration technology? What really is Devops? We help you plan all these and more.

Sustainable Processes

We help you create sustainable processes that will grow with your company and product. How should you implement product development? Should you scrum or kanban? What is CICD? These are process questions that make a huge impact both on team morale and product quality. We help with planning this out.

Due Dilligence

When you need to evaluate external vendors/technical service providers, or even interfacing with CTOs, Chief Architects from other organizations, we can speak and advocate for you to make sure decisions are made that benefit you and your company in the long term.

Investor Meetings

A lot of times, investors will be more comfortable knowing that there is someone with a lot of senior-level experience that stands behind the product. We can be that.

About Us

Jayo was born out of a desire to help fill the technical gap between vision and execution for a lot of startups and some more established businesses. We have experience with helping build startups from idea to product launch, scaling and optimizing the technical capabilities of existing companies in various industries such as Education, Advertising technologies, and cybersecurity.

We believe that lack of a full time technical leader and strategist, or the resources to get one should not prevent a business from validating their product or pleasing their current customer base. We want to be advocates for founders, business owners, who have the vision but not necessarily the technical expertise to execute efficiently on that vision.
